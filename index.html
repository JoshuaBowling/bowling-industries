About

Bowling Industries focuses on saving lives and making our service members' lives a bit easier. While we are NOT officially associated with the US military, we soon hope to be.

Primary research areas: AI missile guidance systems, AI-assisted exoskeleton suits, body armor, jet engines, and robotics.

Selected Research Areas

AI Assisted Missile Guidance System Research

Research

Research on Artificial Intelligence's ability to accurately guide missiles while being mindful of and protecting civilians, Artificial Intelligence's ability to work under rough conditions, And whether AI could still guide missiles within clouds, heavy rain, and warfare conditions.

  • Simulation-based algorithm evaluation
  • Sensor fusion & robustness testing
  • Safety, verification & validation workflows
Status: Prototype simulations and logged experiments.

Wearable Robotics — Assistive Exoskeleton Concepts

Concept & Testing

Conceptual and simulation work on powered exoskeletons intended for mobility assistance and load-bearing support. Emphasis is on human factors, safety, and military applications. No operational or weaponized prototypes are currently being developed.

  • Human-in-the-loop simulation
  • Ergonomics and safe actuation concepts
  • Control algorithms for assistive motion (simulation only)
Status: Concept design, simulation validation pending formal review.
